# 30-Day Content Calendar for Indie Hackers

> Plan a full month of content across platforms. Duplicate this page each month and customize the topics to match your product and audience.

---

## Content Strategy Overview

**Target Audience:** SaaS founders, indie hackers, solo developers, bootstrappers

**Core Content Pillars:**
1. Building in Public (progress updates, milestones, lessons)
2. Technical Tutorials (how you built features, stack decisions)
3. Business & Growth (revenue, marketing, pricing, strategy)
4. Community & Engagement (polls, questions, replies, collaborations)

**Posting Frequency Goals:**
- Twitter/X: 1 post/day (mix of threads and short posts)
- Reddit / Indie Hackers: 2-3 posts/week
- Blog: 1 article/week

## Content Repurposing Plan

Every piece of long-form content (blog articles, long threads) should be repurposed:

| Original Content | Repurpose Into | Platform |
|---|---|---|
| Blog article | Twitter thread (summarize key points) | Twitter/X |
| Blog article | Short post with link | LinkedIn, Indie Hackers |
| Twitter thread | Blog article (expand on points) | Blog |
| Twitter thread | Reddit post (reformat) | Reddit |
| User feedback screenshot | Social proof post | Twitter/X, LinkedIn |
| Customer story | Case study blog post | Blog |
| Product update | Changelog entry + email | Blog, Email |
